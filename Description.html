Catch them all!

PetTracker is a tool designed to help all kinds of trainers in their pet tracking and battling duties:

• Displays the locations of all pets, stables and tamers on the world map.
• Tracks your progress of catching pets in your current zone.
• Exposes pet breeds in combat, the journal and tooltips.
• Shows the breed and rarity of pets in battle and warns you of upgrades.
• Determines and displays all your enemies abilities as you fight.
• Provides a journal of all pet PvE encounters and their teams.
